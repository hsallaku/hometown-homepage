Enjoy the rich culture and hospitality this hidden gem in Albania has to offer.

Top three activities to do at Kruja

Shopping at the Old Bazaar

Whether you need a traditionally-crafted rug, or masterfully-made copper products from the renowned craftsmen who work here, you can find every object of interest you might need! Other items in the fascinating bazaar include genuine handcrafted items, and silver jewellery that is known to last a lifetime.

Sight-seeing around the Kruja Castle

The Kruja Castle overlooks the picturesque town of Kruja. The fortress houses two museums: the Ethnographic Museum and Soviet-era Skanderbeg Museum, containing exhibits on the revered Albanian military leader. Visitors will also find the remains of a mosque, church, and a medieval hammam.

Hiking Mt. Kruja

This hike is considered moderate due to its elevation. You will reach the top of Kruja Mountain 1117m, part of Skanderbeg Mountain Range, starting your walk at approx. 600m above sea level, making your hike intense due to the elevation gain of 500 m in a short distance. Time: 1 h 45 minutes one way. This is a wide trail with very rocky terrain but the reward is amazing - you will enjoy amazing VIEWS throughout this trail!
